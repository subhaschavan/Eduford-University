About Us

We are the World's Largest Teaching Platform

Eduford University is one of the world's leading acadmic and research institutions, offering a unique educational experience that prepares the next generation of global citizens to laed and make a difference in the world. We are a vibrant community of exceptional students, faculty, alumni and Professionals dedicated to real-world applications of our research, scholership, faith and Service. Estabilished in 1989, Eduford is the nation's oldest Catholic and Jesuit University. Drawing upon the 450-year-old legacy of jesuit education. We provide students with a world-class learning experience focused on educating the whole person through exposure to different faiths, cultures and beliefs.
